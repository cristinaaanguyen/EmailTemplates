ClearSight system

The noninvasive ClearSight system provides valuable hemodynamic insight to an expanded patient population, for making more informed decisions about fluid and hypotension management in moderate- to high-risk surgical patients.1-3
The ClearSight finger cuff technology is compatible with the EV1000 clinical platform.

FloTrac system

The FloTrac system is the practical, reliable solution used and trusted by clinicans more than other minimally-invasive volume management solution worldwide.

Discover our first-of-its-kind predictive hypotension management software.

Hypotension Prediction Index software detects the likelihood of a hypotensive event* before the event occurs, and provides you with insights to understand  the root cause and inform a potential course of action for your patient.


*A hypotensive event is defined as MAP < 65 mmHg for at least one minute duration.
